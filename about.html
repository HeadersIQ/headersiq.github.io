About

HeadersIQ is a research effort led by Marcelo Valentim Silva at Curtin University. It focuses on header-centric semantic type detection and data quality assessment using an explainable pipeline and the HeadersIQ metric.

Short Bio

Marcelo is a PhD candidate at Curtin University working on semantic type detection and data quality. He has extensive experience in data governance and quality assessment, and contributes open artefacts and benchmarks to the community.
